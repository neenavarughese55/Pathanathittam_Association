Pathanamthitta

Where Tradition Meets Tranquility

Founded 1st November 1982

Pathanamthitta, situated in Kerala, South India, serves as the administrative hub of the Pathanamthitta district. Nestled near the Western Ghats, this region boasts a picturesque landscape characterised by gentle hills, verdant forests, meandering rivers, and idyllic countryside. Renowned for its vibrant fairs and festivals, the district is often hailed as the 'Capital of Pilgrimage Tourism' in Kerala.

Noteworthy for its communal and cultural harmony, Pathanamthitta is home to a multitude of churches, temples, and mosques coexisting harmoniously.

There are four municipalities in Pathanamthitta: Tiruvalla, Adoor, Pathanamthitta and Pandalam.
